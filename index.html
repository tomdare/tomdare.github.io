The Ultimate Bulking Sandwich

Ingredients

1x White Sourdough Baguette

12x Tbsp Lloyd Grossman Roasted Garlic and Tomato Pasta Sauce

5x Chicken Thighs

150g Mozzarella

6x Prosciutto Slices

25g El Cierzo Chorizo Slices

Arugula

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Italian Herb Seasoning

Balsamic Vinaigrette

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1

Turn your grill on to the highest heat and drizzle some olive oil into a large frying pan

Step 2

Slice 100-200g of White Sourdough Baguette depending on your preference, slice it in half lengthways and lightly drizzle in olive oil.

Step 3

Grab your chicken thighs, trim the excess fat off if not already done so, and generously coat both sides in the Italian Herb Seasoning. Once this is complete, turn on your frying pan to the highest heat and let it warm up.

Step 4

Now your grill is warm, place the sourdough in to allow a light crisp on the bread before any future steps.

Step 5

Now your pan is warm but the oil is NOT burnt, turn it down to a medium heat and gently place the thighs into the pan.

Step 6

Remove the lightly crisped bread from the grill (NO BLACK EDGES, just golden brown) and add 6x Tbsp of Tomato Sauce to each side and coat evenly across the bread. Once an even finish is applied, place it back under the grill.

Step 7

Grab your Mozzarella cheese, drain ut from the bag if need be, and slice it into a dozen thin pieces.

Step 8

Flip the chicken once each side has a golden brown crisp.

Step 9

Remove the bread from the grill and place half the mozzarella to each side, ensuring it spans the full length of the bread.

Step 10

While the mozzarella is melting, slice the thickest part of a chicken thigh. If its white throughout, turn off the frying pan and place all the thighs on a clean plate.

Step 11

Once the mozzarella has some touches of brown and is fully melted, take your Chorizo and Prosciutto slices and apply each to one side of the bread.

Step 12

Remove the sandwich from the grill once the meats have just started to crisp (NOT CRISPED THROUGHOUT) and turn off the grill.

Step 13

Place the rested chicken thighs on one side of the sandwhich and top it off with some handfulls of Arugula

FINAL STEP: Step 14

Drizzle a light amount of Olive Oil and Balsamic Vinaigrette on one side, then place the other side on top of it and push down. Now sit back, take photos and admire the creation you have just miracled up.

Alternative Recipes

Salad

DIFFERENCE: Replace the bread with Italian Rosa Verde Salad and choose Buffalo Mozzarella over normal. DO NOT MELT THE MOZZARELLA, just slice into small pieces. Grill the Prosciutto and Chorizo on baking paper.
